Our Services

Whether it’s an intimate elopement or a grand celebration, we design and manage events with grace and intention.

Full Service Planning

Designed with heart, delivered with care.We believe every celebration should feel effortless and deeply personal. Our full-service planning is built around your story, your style, and your peace of mind.

  • Vision development and theme styling
  • Venue scouting and vendor coordination
  • Budget creation and timeline management
  • Guest list management and RSVP tracking
  • Day-of execution and guest experience oversight

Partial Planning

Whether you're halfway there or just need a second set of eyes, we step in with clarity, creativity, and calm to elevate your event without taking over.

  • Vendor recommendations and contract review
  • Styling consultation and layout refinement
  • Timeline polishing and day-of coordination
  • Budget assessment and optimization
  • Guest communication support
  • Creative enhancements and branding support

Custom Decor & Themes

We transform your vision into a sensory experience guests will remember. Whether you're dreaming of elegance, whimsy, or bold innovation, we design every detail to reflect your story and elevate your brand.

  • Bespoke theme development and mood boards

  • Interactive elements for educational or kid-friendly events
